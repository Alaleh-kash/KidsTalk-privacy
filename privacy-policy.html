KidsTalk – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

KidsTalk is a bilingual educational app for young children. We respect your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

1. No Personal Data Collected

KidsTalk does NOT collect:

  • Names or email addresses
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising IDs

2. No Ads & No Tracking

The app contains:

  • No advertisements
  • No tracking
  • No analytics services
  • No data sharing

3. Child-Safe App

KidsTalk is designed for children and follows:

  • Google Play Families Policy
  • COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act)

4. Internet Access

The app may use the internet only for:

  • Downloading internal voice resources
  • Checking for updates

No personal data is transmitted or stored.
